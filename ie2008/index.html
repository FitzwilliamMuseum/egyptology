IAE Computer Group Meeting 2008

Thanks to the kindness of Dr Wilfried Seipel, the meeting took place in the Kunsthistorisches Museum, Vienna, Austria, on 8-11 July 2008, with the sessions on 9-10 July. There were approximately 40 participants.

All members of our group are very grateful to our hosts, above all Regina Hölzl and her colleagues, for making the meeting go so smoothly.

There were an interesting range of papers presented, and a publication is envisaged. At present we are awaiting formal gudelines from the publisher. In the meantime, it was suggested that PDF and PPT files of the presentations be collected on this site. They can be accessed from the link below; they will just appear as a list of files in a directory, and clicking on the required one will download it.
