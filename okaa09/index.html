Old Kingdom Art and Archaeology 2009

The Old Kingdom Art and Archaeology group met on 20-23 May 2009 at the Fitzwilliam Museum, Cambridge.

Please find at the side links to information from the conference.

The proceedings of the conference will be published by Oxbow Books. The working title is Old Kingdom: New Perspectives. Egyptian Art and Archaeology 2750-2150 BC. Further information will be made available on this web site in the near future.

We would like to thank everyone who came to Cambridge and made the meeting a success.
