Old Kingdom Art and Archaeology 2009

Where will the conference take place?

OKAA09 will take place in the Seminar Room on the Lower Ground Floor of the Fitzwilliam Museum. Please enter the museum by the Courtyard Entrance at the south end of the museum (the modern entrance), and signs will take you to the room.

Registration for the conference will be possible from 10:00 on Wednesday 20 May.

The Saturday sessions will take place in the Zoology Theatre; you will be provided with directions at the conference.

Where is the Fitzwilliam Museum?

The Fitzwilliam Museum is the art museum of the University of Cambridge, and is centrally located. The address is:

The Fitzwilliam Museum
Trumpington Street,
Cambridge CB2 1RB

http://www.fitzmuseum.cam.ac.uk/
