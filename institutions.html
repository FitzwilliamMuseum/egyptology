Egyptological Institutions

Links updated 8 January 2017

Most universities and institutions of course now have WWW pages. Thus I have tried to select some of those which might be of use to readers of Egyptology Resources. Museums of course have their own page on this site. These links will usually go to the Egyptology sub-pages within an institution, or the division of that institution which teaches or undertakes research in Egyptology. The non-appearance of an institution here is no comment on that institution's standing--it purely indicates that I am not yet aware that it has a substantive web page.

There is also the EEF list of Egyptological Societies and Institutes.

Many of these pages are in the languages of the country concerned.
