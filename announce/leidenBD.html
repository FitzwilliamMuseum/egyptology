On the 4th of November 2002 a symposium on the Book of the Dead will be held at the University of Leiden, organised by the Department of Egyptology in collaboration with the CNWS:

10.15 – 11.15 Irmtraut Munro (Bonn): - The Book of the Dead Project at Bonn University (short presentation).
- A recently Unrolled Papyrus with Amuletic and Book of the Dead Spells.

11.15 – 12.15 Holger Kockelmann (Bonn): The Book of the Dead on the Mummy Bandages of the Memphite Priest Hor: M. Berlin P. 3073 + M. London BM 10265-10266 + M. New York Amherst 41.

14.00 – 15.00 Mathieu Heerma van Voss (Amsterdam): Beginning and End in the Book of the Dead of the 21st Dynasty.

15.00 - 16.00 Giuseppina Lenzo (Geneva): Some Book of the Dead hieratic papyri of the Third Intermediate Period in the Egyptian Museum of Turin.

16.15 – 17.15 Rita Lucarelli (Leiden): Defensive Magic in the Book of the Dead: the Spells for Warding Off Evil.

For further information: r.lucarelli@let.leidenuniv.nl
