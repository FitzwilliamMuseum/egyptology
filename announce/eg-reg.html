Collections Management System Supervisor and Assistant, Egyptian Museum Database Project, Cairo (updated 4 April 08)

 

The American Research Center in Egypt (ARCE) is looking for two Egyptologists, one senior and one junior, to work with the Egyptian Museum Database Project. Responsibilities for both candidates would include transcription and verification of information from the museum’s register books into the current Filemaker database. The senior candidate will also: assist the project director in preparing to migrate data from this database to a new system; will help supervise the actual data migration; will be trained in the use of the new software; and then will assist in the training of the museum staff. These positions would begin between April and June of 2008, depending on the availability of the successful candidates, and run for one year each, with an option to renew for a second year. Solid Egyptological expertise is needed, as well as facility with computers and databases. Interested candidates should send a cover letter and CV to the Project Director, Dr. Janice Kamrin, at jkamrin@gmail.com.
