.         7TH INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE OF DEMOTIC STUDIES                 Copenhagen, August 23-27, 1999                         FIRST CIRCULARDear Colleagues,As decided at the meeting of the 6th International Conference ofDemotic Studies in Cairo in 1996, the next conference will take placein Copenhagen from August 23 through 27, 1999, under the auspices ofthe University of Copenhagen.The Conference will be organised by the Carsten Niebuhr Institute ofNear Eastern Studies and registration will take place on the morningof the 23rd of August.There will be an excursion to some interesting sites on the island ofZeeland, and opportunity will be provided for visits to the localmuseum collections.PAPERSThose wishing to present a paper are asked to submit the title and anabstract (see the enclosed formula') not later than April 31, 1999.All speakers are asked to adhere to a limit of 40 minutes, to allowsufficient time for a discussion period and a break.There is no theme for the conference as a whole, but given the natureof the contents of the collection of papyri in Copenhagen (theCarlsberg Papyri), it is hoped that due attention will be given tothe study of literary texts.The languages of the conference will be English, French and German,but abstracts and written papers may be submitted in other languages.REGISTRATION AND HOTEL RESERVATIONSThe registration fee will be the equivalent of US $ 15.August is the peak of the tourist season in Copenhagen, and we wouldtherefore ask those interested to return the attached registrationform no later than September 1, 1998. Copenhagen offers a wide rangeof accomodations, but since nearly all categories are in great demandrooms will be reserved on a first-come, first-served basis. Incentral Copenhagen a single Bed & Breakfast may be had for US $ 65, adouble for US $ 85. Rooms without bath may be had for less. A list ofmoderately priced restaurants (c. 8-15 US $) will be issued to theparticipants.We are looking forward to seeing all of you in Copenhagen.Paul John Frandsen & Kim RyholtCopenhagen, June 1, 1998            ----------------------------------------     SEPTIEME RENCONTRE INTERNATIONALE DES ETUDES DEMOTIQUES                 Copenhague, 23-27 AouT 1999                     PREMIERE CIRCULAIREChers collegues,Suite aux deliberations de la Sixieme Rencontre Internationale auCaire (1996), la prochaine Rencontre Internationale des EtudesDemotistiques se tiendra a Copenhague du 23 au 29 aout 1999, sous lesauspices de l'Universite de Copenhague.La rencontre sera organisee par l'Institut Carsten Niebuhr pour lesEtudes du Proche Orient et l'inscription aura lieu dans le matin du23 aout 1999.Une excursion est prevue dans des localites du Seeland, et pendant larencontre il y aura la possibilite de visiter les collections desmuseums locaux.COMMUNICATIONSLes participants ayant l'intention de presenter une communication sonpries d'en indiquer le titre et ou un abstrait avant le 31 avril1999. La duree de chaque communication ne doit pas exceder 40minutes.Il n'y a pas de theme general de la rencontre, mais les organisateursaimeraient bien une certaine insistance sur le texte litteraire,etant donne la composition des papyrus de notre collection (Papyrusde Carlsberg).Les communications se feront en francais, en anglais ou en allemand,mais evidemment on acceptera aussi des abstraits ou des communi-cations ecrits en d'autres langues.INSCRIPTIONS ET HEBERGEMENTLes frais d'inscription seront l'equivalent de US $ 15.Etant donne l'important afflux touristique a Copenhague, nous prionstoutes les personnes interessees de nous renvoyer au plus vite leformulaire ci-inclus, au plus tard le 1 septembre 1998. Le prix prevud'une chambre avec petit dejeuner s'estime a US $ 65 pour unepersonne, 85 pour deux personnes.Dans l'espoir d'une reponse positive, nous vous prions de croire al'assurance de nos sentiments les meilleurs.Paul John Frandsen & Kim RyholtCopenhague, juin 1, 1998               ----------------------------------------            7TH INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE OF DEMOTIC STUDIES                    Copenhagen, August 23-27, 1999                          REGISTRATION FORMI intend to participate in the 7th International Conference ofDemotic Studies in Copenhagen 1999.Name:Title:Address (private or institutional as preferred)Institution:Street:City:ZIP code:State/Country:Telephone:Fax:E-Mail:I intend to give a paper: yes / noProvisional title or subject:I intend to participate in the excursion: yes / no(Details of the excursion will be provided in a later circular)Date:Signature:Please return this registration form before 1.
