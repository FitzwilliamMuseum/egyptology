The Research School of Asian, African and American Studies (CNWS), in collaboration with the Department of Egyptology of Leiden University is very pleased to invite you to attend a lecture by

CYNTHIA MAY SHEIKHOLESLAMI : Aspects of the Theology of Montu, Lord of Thebes

on Wednesday 23 April 2003, 11.00-13.00 hrs in room 152, bld 1175, Cleveringaplaats 1, Leiden

The lecture will provide a review of aspects of the development of the theology of Montu, god of the Theban nome, from the early Middle Kingdom to the Late Period, with particular focus on Montu’s relationships with the deities Hathor, Amun, Khonsu, and Harsomtus.

For further information please contact: r.lucarelli@let.leidenuniv.nl
