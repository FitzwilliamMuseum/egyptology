FINAL CALL FOR PAPERS for the

SSEAscholars' colloquium 2001

This is a final reminder that The Society for the Study of Egyptian Antiquities will hold their Scholars' Colloquium in conjunction with its Annual Symposium on November 9th, 2001*. The SSEA invites submissions for 20-minute papers from senior scholars and from graduate students in Egyptology or related fields. The proposed papers may be on ANY facet of the study of ancient Egypt, providing that the work presented represents original research. Please submit your abstract to ssea@bigfoot.com, with attention to Dr. Lyn Green.A vetting process will be in place, supervised by Prof. R.J. Leprohon.

Deadline for submissions will be October 15th, 2001.

The Scholars' Colloquium will take place on Friday, November 9th, 2001. Location: Royal Ontario Museum Lecture Theatre, 100 Queen's Park Cres. [To avoid difficulties, please enter the museum via the South/School Entrance, adjacent to the Children's Own Museum.].

Time: 10:15 am to 5 pm

Admission to the Scholars' Colloquium is free and open to all.

* The Annual Public Symposium is scheduled to take place on Saturday, November 10th at the Royal Ontario Museum. The topic of this year's Symposium is "Cleopatra's Egypt". Submissions for the Scholars' Colloquium are not limited to the theme of the Symposium. Student cost for the Saturday event is $50/$45 for SSEA members
