Museums with Egyptian Collections

Links updated 7 August 2018

Most museums now have a web presence. I have tried to select some of those which have more specific information on their Egyptian Collections, but I do also include the general web presences of major museums with relevant material.In general, I am now directing links to the home page of the museum in question, as they tend to be stable, while the Egyptian-specific links can be variable. Some links may not have the official blessing of the institution concerned, but seem reliable.

It is normal for a museum's web page to be in the language of the country concerned, but I do note some other language pages.

There is a more detailed list of museums with Egyptian objects by Sven Moons at http://www.desheret.org/museum.html

The developing CIPEG web site will also give more information.
