Journals, Serials and Magazines

1 August 2018

These web sites are a mixture of those which just tell you about the publication in question, how to subscribe etc, and those which actually contain online articles. In some cases, only part of a series is available online, often via an archiving or out of copyright scanning service.

  • Journals and series
  • Magazines
  • Newsletters
  • Egyptian newspapers and news sites (online editions)

The Ancient World Online has a list of what it terms open-access journals. These are a mixture of those which have some online, and which are all online--and of course it is wider than Egyptology. A number are digitised versions of old and hard to find journals.
